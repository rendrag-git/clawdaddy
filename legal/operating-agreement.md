**3.3 Standard of Care**

## Article 5: Distributions

**5.1 Authority**  
The Member may authorize distributions of cash or property at such times and in such amounts as the Member determines, subject to applicable law.

**5.2 Limitations**

- The Company would be unable to pay its debts as they become due in the ordinary course of business, or
- The Company's total assets would be less than its total liabilities

**5.3 Tax Distributions**

## Article 9: Death, Incapacity, and Succession

**9.1 Continuity Upon Death or Incapacity**  
The death, disability, or incapacity of the Member shall not cause the dissolution of the Company. The Company shall continue in existence and shall be managed by the Member's personal representative, executor, administrator, guardian, conservator, or successor-in-interest ("Successor"), who shall have all rights and powers of the Member under this Agreement.

**9.2 Designated Successor**  
The Member may designate a successor manager or member by written instrument delivered to the Company's records. In the absence of such designation, the Member's interest shall pass according to the Member's estate plan or applicable law.

**9.3 Transfer of Control**  
The Successor shall assume management of the Company within ninety (90) days of the Member's death or adjudication of incapacity. During any interim period, the Company's operations shall continue under any existing contracts, and any authorized agent or employee may take actions reasonably necessary to preserve Company assets and ongoing obligations to customers.

## Article 11: Dissolution and Winding Up

**11.1 Events of Dissolution**  
The Company shall be dissolved upon:
- The written decision of the Member to dissolve the Company
- Entry of a decree of judicial dissolution under Ohio law
- Any other event requiring dissolution under Ohio law

**11.2 Winding Up**  
Upon dissolution, the Member (or a person designated by the Member) shall wind up the Company's affairs, including:
- Collecting Company assets
- Discharging Company liabilities
- Distributing remaining assets to the Member

**11.3 Termination**  
The Company shall terminate when all assets have been distributed and Articles of Dissolution have been filed with the Ohio Secretary of State.

1. Introduction

ClawDaddy LLC ("ClawDaddy," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our managed hosting services for OpenClaw AI assistants ("Service").

By using our Service, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

This is a starting-point privacy policy and has not been reviewed by legal counsel. We recommend having it reviewed by a qualified attorney before using it in production.

2. What We Do

ClawDaddy provides managed hosting for AI assistants. This means:

  • We host your conversations with your AI assistant on our infrastructure.
  • We store your data (messages, configurations, files, and other content you create through the Service).
  • We process your interactions to deliver the AI assistant functionality.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, billing information, and payment details.
  • Service Data: Conversations, messages, files, configurations, and any other content you create, upload, or transmit through your AI assistant.
  • Support Communications: Messages you send us for customer support or inquiries.

3.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the Service, such as access times, features used, and errors encountered.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system (only when necessary for Service operation or security).

3.3 AI Interaction Data (Fully Managed Tier)

If you use our Fully Managed tier, your AI assistant conversations are transmitted to third-party AI API providers through ClawDaddy's API credentials. This means:

  • Your conversation data passes through our API account with the AI provider.
  • The AI provider may process, log, or retain your conversation data subject to their privacy policy and terms of service.
  • ClawDaddy does not control and is not responsible for the AI provider's data handling practices.
  • We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of the applicable AI provider (e.g., Anthropic, OpenAI) for details on how they handle data submitted through their APIs.

For Managed BYOK tier customers, conversations are transmitted using your own API credentials, and the AI provider's data handling is governed by your direct relationship with them.

3.4 What We DON'T Collect (Currently)

  • We do not use tracking cookies or analytics on our landing page. We may add privacy-respecting analytics in the future, and we'll update this policy if we do.
  • We do not collect sensitive personal information unless you voluntarily provide it through your use of the Service.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the Service: Host your AI assistant, process your requests, and deliver functionality.
  • Maintain and improve the Service: Fix bugs, optimize performance, and develop new features.
  • Provide customer support: Respond to your questions and troubleshoot issues.
  • Process payments: Bill you for the Service and manage your subscription.
  • Comply with legal obligations: Respond to legal requests or prevent fraud and abuse.

5. We Don't Read Your Conversations (Except When Necessary)

Your conversations with your AI assistant are private. We do not routinely access, read, or monitor your conversations.

We may access your data only when:

  • You request support: If you contact us for help with a technical issue, we may need to review relevant logs or messages to diagnose the problem.
  • Debugging or troubleshooting: If there's a service issue affecting your account, we may need to inspect data to identify the cause.
  • Security or abuse prevention: If we detect potential security threats, violations of our Terms of Service, or illegal activity, we may investigate as necessary.
  • Legal requirement: If required by law, court order, or legal process.

We will access your data only to the extent necessary and with appropriate safeguards.

6. We Don't Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or conversation data to third parties. Period.

7. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

7.1 Service Providers

We may share data with third-party service providers who help us operate the Service, such as:

  • Cloud hosting providers (to store your data)
  • Payment processors (to handle billing)
  • AI API providers (to deliver AI assistant functionality)

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

7.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights or property
  • Prevent fraud or abuse
  • Protect the safety of our users or the public

7.3 Business Transfers

If ClawDaddy is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or a prominent notice on our website before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

8. Data Retention

8.1 Active Accounts

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and as necessary to provide the Service.

8.2 After Cancellation

When you cancel your account, your data will be deleted within 30 days. This includes:

  • Your conversations and messages
  • Configuration files and settings
  • Uploaded files and content

We may retain some data for longer periods if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., billing records, fraud prevention).

8.3 Backups

Deleted data may persist in backups for a limited time, but these backups are not accessible for normal use and are eventually purged according to our backup retention schedule.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

However, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and you use the Service at your own risk.

9.1 Breach Notification

In the event of a security breach that results in unauthorized access to your personal information, we will:

  • Notify affected users within seventy-two (72) hours of becoming aware of the breach, or as otherwise required by applicable law.
  • Provide details about the nature of the breach, the data affected, and the steps we are taking to remediate the issue.
  • Comply with all applicable breach notification laws, including Ohio's data breach notification statute (ORC Â§ 1349.19).

10. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

10.1 Access and Correction

You can access and update most of your account information through your account settings. If you need help accessing or correcting your information, contact us at pearson@clawdaddy.sh.

10.2 Data Portability

You may request a copy of your data in a portable format. We'll work with you to provide your data in a reasonable format.

10.3 Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through your account settings. Upon deletion, your data will be removed within 30 days as described in Section 8.

10.4 Opt-Out

If we add marketing emails or newsletters in the future, you will have the ability to opt out. Currently, we only send transactional emails related to your account and Service usage.

11. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. As specified in our Terms of Service, you must be at least 18 years of age to use the Service. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that we have collected information from an individual under 18 without appropriate legal authorization, we will delete it promptly.

12. California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Know: You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the sources, the purposes, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing: We do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. If this changes, we will provide a "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA/CPRA rights.

To exercise these rights, contact us at pearson@clawdaddy.sh. We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days as required by law.

Categories of personal information we collect (as defined by the CCPA): Identifiers (name, email, IP address); commercial information (purchase history, subscription tier); internet/electronic activity (usage data, logs); and any personal information you voluntarily provide through your AI assistant conversations.

13. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. If you access the Service from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to, stored, and processed in the U.S. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification (if you have an active account)

Your continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

15. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: pearson@clawdaddy.sh

We'll do our best to respond to your inquiry promptly.

FAQ

What is OpenClaw?

An open-source AI assistant that lives on a server and connects to your chat apps. It can check your email, manage your calendar, write code, browse the web, and remember everything across conversations.

What do I need to run this myself?

Any Mac (Intel or Apple Silicon), Windows PC (10/11 with WSL), or Linux machine. Even that old laptop collecting dust works ‚Äî OpenClaw is lightweight. Leave it plugged in and connected to WiFi, and you've got a 24/7 AI assistant. A $5/mo VPS works too if you'd rather run it in the cloud.

Do I need to keep my computer on?

Yes ‚Äî your assistant lives on that machine. If it sleeps, your assistant sleeps. That's why we offer managed plans ‚Äî we keep it running 24/7 so you don't have to.

Why not just set it up myself for free?

You absolutely can. OpenClaw is open source. ClawDaddy exists for people who want the faster path: quiz-based setup, dedicated hosting, and less time debugging random config issues.

Is my data safe?

Your instance is yours alone ‚Äî dedicated server, not shared. We never access your data, conversations, or API keys. You get full VNC access to your machine.

What if I want to leave?

Export your data anytime. Cancel and we keep your instance running for 7 days so you can migrate. No lock-in, no guilt trips.

What's an API key?

It's like a password that lets your assistant talk to the AI. You get one from Anthropic (the company behind Claude) or OpenAI. Takes about 2 minutes. Starter, Pro BYOK, and Power BYOK all use your own key for full transparency over costs.

Can I re-run the install script?

If you're self-hosting OpenClaw separately, yes. For ClawDaddy plans we handle updates and instance maintenance for you.

Is ClawDaddy available now?

Yes. ClawDaddy is live and available now. You can start with the quiz and pick a plan right away.

Starter, Pro, and Power are monthly and can be canceled anytime, with time to export your data.

All live plans include automatic runtime and infrastructure updates.

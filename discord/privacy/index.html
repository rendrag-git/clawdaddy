1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ClawDaddy LLC ("ClawDaddy," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you interact with a ClawDaddy AI assistant bot ("Bot") on Discord.

This policy applies to all Discord users who interact with the Bot, whether or not they are ClawDaddy customers. If you are also a ClawDaddy subscriber, our main Privacy Policy governs the ClawDaddy hosting platform. This policy governs only the Bot's operation within Discord.

2. What the Bot Collects

2.1 Data Collected Through Discord's API

When the Bot is active in a Discord server, it may receive the following data through Discord's API, based on the permissions granted by the server administrator:

  • Message content: Text of messages in channels where the Bot has access. The Bot processes messages to generate responses.
  • User identifiers: Discord user ID, username, display name, and avatar (as provided by Discord's API).
  • Server identifiers: Server (guild) ID, server name, channel IDs, and channel names.
  • Message metadata: Timestamps, message IDs, and reply/thread context.
  • Role information: Your roles within the server (used for permission checks and context).

2.2 Data the Bot Does NOT Collect

  • Direct messages: The Bot does not read your DMs unless you directly message the Bot.
  • Voice data: The Bot does not join voice channels or record audio.
  • Messages in other servers: The Bot only accesses data in servers where it has been added and granted permissions.
  • Your Discord password or authentication tokens.
  • Messages in channels where the Bot has not been granted access.

3. How the Bot Uses Your Data

The Bot uses the data it receives to:

  • Generate responses: Message content is sent to a third-party AI model provider to generate the Bot's replies.
  • Maintain conversation context: Recent messages may be retained temporarily to provide coherent, context-aware responses within a conversation.
  • Operate Bot features: Slash commands, message reactions, thread participation, and other Bot functionality.

The Bot does not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing the Bot's functionality.

4. Third-Party AI Model Providers

The Bot generates responses by sending message content to third-party AI model providers. Currently, the Bot may use:

  • Anthropic (Claude models)
  • OpenAI (GPT models)

Message content sent to these providers is subject to their respective privacy policies and data handling practices:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

ClawDaddy operates on a Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) model. The Bot Owner (the ClawDaddy customer who deployed the Bot) provides their own API key. Conversation data passes through the Bot Owner's API account with the model provider.

Important: When you send a message in a channel where the Bot is active, that message may be transmitted to a third-party AI provider for processing. By interacting with the Bot, you acknowledge this data flow.

5. Data Storage and Retention

5.1 Conversation Data

The Bot may store conversation history to provide context for ongoing interactions. This data is stored on the Bot Owner's dedicated ClawDaddy instance.

  • Active conversations: Message context is retained for the duration configured by the Bot Owner (typically recent messages within a session or thread).
  • Persistent memory: The Bot may retain summaries or notes from conversations in its memory files, stored on the Bot Owner's instance.
  • Logs: Message logs may be retained on the Bot Owner's instance for debugging and quality control.

5.2 Where Data Is Stored

Conversation data is stored on the Bot Owner's ClawDaddy instance, hosted on infrastructure in the region selected by the Bot Owner (US or EU). ClawDaddy does not maintain a separate central database of Discord conversations.

5.3 Retention Period

Conversation data is retained for as long as the Bot Owner maintains their ClawDaddy account. When the Bot Owner cancels their account, all data on their instance is deleted within 30 days, as described in ClawDaddy's main Privacy Policy.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or conversation data.

Your data may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • Third-party AI providers: Message content is sent to AI model providers to generate responses (see Section 4).
  • The Bot Owner: The Bot Owner (who deployed the Bot to their server) has access to conversation data on their ClawDaddy instance, including logs and memory files.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Opt Out of Bot Interaction

You can avoid the Bot processing your messages by:

  • Not sending messages in channels where the Bot is active.
  • Asking the server administrator to restrict the Bot's access to specific channels.
  • Leaving the server.

7.2 Data Deletion Requests

If you want your data removed from the Bot's memory or logs, you may:

  • Contact the Bot Owner (the server administrator who added the Bot) and request they delete your data from the Bot's instance.
  • Contact ClawDaddy at hello@clawdaddy.sh and we will coordinate with the Bot Owner.

7.3 GDPR Rights (EEA/UK Users)

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the following rights regarding personal data the Bot processes:

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@clawdaddy.sh. We will respond within 30 days.

Note on data controller/processor roles: The Bot Owner is the data controller for conversation data processed in their server. ClawDaddy acts as a data processor on behalf of the Bot Owner. GDPR requests may require coordination between ClawDaddy and the Bot Owner.

8. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by Discord's Terms of Service in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the Bot, contact us at hello@clawdaddy.sh and we will delete it.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data processed by the Bot, including:

  • Encryption in transit (all data between Discord, the Bot, and AI providers is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS)
  • Isolated instances per Bot Owner (no shared databases between customers)
  • Access controls on Bot Owner instances

No system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data processed by the Bot.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Material changes will be posted to our website. Continued interaction with the Bot after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

FAQ

What is OpenClaw?

An open-source AI assistant that lives on a server and connects to your chat apps. It can check your email, manage your calendar, write code, browse the web, and remember everything across conversations. It's like having a really smart intern who never sleeps.

What do I need to run this myself?

Any Mac (Intel or Apple Silicon), Windows PC (10/11 with WSL), or Linux machine. Even that old laptop collecting dust works ‚Äî OpenClaw is lightweight. Leave it plugged in and connected to WiFi, and you've got a 24/7 AI assistant. A $5/mo VPS works too if you'd rather run it in the cloud.

Do I need to keep my computer on?

Yes ‚Äî your assistant lives on that machine. If it sleeps, your assistant sleeps. That's why we offer managed plans ‚Äî we keep it running 24/7 so you don't have to.

Why not just set it up myself for free?

You absolutely can! OpenClaw is open source. Our $49 script just saves you the afternoon of googling error messages. And if you'd rather skip the terminal entirely, our managed plans exist.

Is my data safe?

Your instance is yours alone ‚Äî dedicated server, not shared. We never access your data, conversations, or API keys. You get full VNC access to your machine.

What if I want to leave?

Export your data anytime. Cancel and we keep your instance running for 7 days so you can migrate. No lock-in, no guilt trips.

Can I use Opus instead of Sonnet?

On the Fully Managed plan, add Opus for +$25/mo. On BYOK and Self-Install, use whatever model your API key supports.

What's an API key?

It's like a password that lets your assistant talk to the AI. You get one from Anthropic (the company behind Claude) at console.anthropic.com. Takes 2 minutes. Our Fully Managed plan skips this ‚Äî we provide the AI.
